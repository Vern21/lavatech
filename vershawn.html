Vershawn Bussell

Vershawn Bussell born to the South side of Chicago is the eldest of three boys. Vershawn always had a passion for video gaming. In the 8th grade Vershawn wanted to go to school for animation art and design. Once in high school he gained a passion for working with kids and wanting to be a teacher. In route to graduating high school he decided to attend Harold Washington College where he attended for one semester. After leaving Harold Washington, he took off a year from school to work and determinate what he should focus his career field studies in. While working at Chipotle, on his way home from work he saw an ad for a program called “Year Up”. He then took that information down, applied, and the rest is History.
